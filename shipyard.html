Shipyard
Shipyard is a site that is dedicated to the most influential and iconic ships in film, video game, and comic history. In creating this concept, I was unsure in how I could create a site that would immulate my love for the Science Fiction genre, looking through countless wiki's and Fictional encyclopedias. In my search I found that vehicles, in particular space ships, did not have their own Index in which others could learn about their history. With Shipyard, I have assembled as much about the history that I could find from all ranges of science fiction staples and brought them here, for the enjoyment and education of those who share a love for this genre as much as I do, thank you!
